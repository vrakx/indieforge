# About TechDirectory

TechDirectory is a curated resource for creators, freelancers, and solopreneurs who want honest, in-depth reviews of the SaaS tools they use every day.

## Our Mission

The software landscape for solopreneurs is overwhelming. Every tool claims to be "the best." We cut through the noise with hands-on testing and comparison articles that actually help you decide.

## How We Work

### No Sponsored Placements
We don't accept paid placements. A tool's position in our comparisons is determined by its actual merits — not how much they pay for advertising.

### Affiliate Revenue, Not Ads
We earn revenue through affiliate links (Systeme.io pays 60% recurring, Kajabi 30%, etc.). This means:
- **You get the same content whether you click our links or not**
- **Our incentives align with yours** — we want tools that work well so you stick around and keep paying
- **No display ads** — clean reading experience, no pop-ups

### Hands-On Testing
Every tool we review is tested for at least 2 weeks before being written about. We test the free tiers, the paid features, and the customer support experience.

## What We Cover

- **Funnel builders** — Systeme.io, Kajabi, ClickFunnels, Carrd
- **Email marketing** — ConvertKit (Kit), MailerLite, Beehiiv
- **Course platforms** — Teachable, Thinkific, Podia
- **AI tools** — ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, Jasper, Midjourney
- **E-commerce** — Shopify apps, Printful, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy
- **Productivity** — Notion, ClickUp, Linear, Reclaim.ai

## The Team

TechDirectory is built by a team of solopreneurs who actually use the tools we review. We're creators, freelancers, and indie hackers — not enterprise tech reviewers. Our perspective is practical: does this tool help you run your business better?
