## The Short Answer

**Systeme.io** is the better choice if you're on a budget ($0/mo free plan) and need an all-in-one platform. **Kajabi** wins if you want premium design, built-in community features, and don't mind paying $149/mo+.

This comparison covers pricing, templates, email marketing, course creation, automation, and real limitations of each platform based on hands-on testing.

## Feature-by-Feature Breakdown

### Course Creation
Both platforms support video lessons, quizzes, and downloads. 

**Kajabi wins on:** Native community features (groups, discussions), polished student experience, mobile app for students.

**Systeme.io wins on:** Price (free plan includes courses), simplicity of course builder.

### Email Marketing
**Systeme.io:** Built-in email marketing included in all plans. Drag-and-drop editor, automation workflows, segmentation.

**Kajabi:** Built-in email marketing with automation. Slightly more polished templates but fewer advanced segments on lower tiers.

**Verdict:** Tie for basic use. Systeme.io has better value; Kajabi has slightly prettier default templates.

### Funnels & Landing Pages
Both platforms include funnel builders. 

**Kajabi's Pipeline Builder** is more visual and intuitive. Templates are premium-looking out of the box.

**Systeme.io's Funnel Builder** covers all the essentials but looks a bit more "template-y." You'll spend time customizing to get a unique look.

### Automation
**Systeme.io:** Zapier integration (paid plans), native email automations, basic workflow triggers.

**Kajabi:** Native automation builder with visual flow editor, Kajabi integrations ecosystem, API access.
