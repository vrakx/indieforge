## Priority Legend

- **🟢 Green** = Low-hanging fruit. Good volume with manageable difficulty (KD ≤5). Prioritize for Phase 1 content.
- **🟡 Yellow** = Moderate opportunity. Higher volume or slightly higher difficulty. Target in Phase 2.
- **🔴 Red** = High competition, high volume. Save for later when domain authority is stronger.

## Top Targets by Category

### Invoicing & Billing (5 keywords, priority: green)
Focus on Wave, FreshBooks, QuickBooks Self-Employed comparisons. Free invoicing roundups are high-volume entry points.

### Contracts & Proposals (5 keywords, mixed)
PandaDoc is the hot brand right now — review content will capture buyer intent. Contract template roundup has high volume but needs strong backlink strategy.

### Project Management (6 keywords, yellow-heavy)
This is the most competitive sub-category. Target long-tail angles: "ClickUp for freelancers," "Notion project management template freelancer."

### Time Tracking & Timesheets (4 keywords, mixed)
Toggl Track and Clockify have strong review search volume. Mobile timesheet apps are an underserved niche.

### CRM & Expenses (5 keywords, green-heavy)
HubSpot free CRM + freelance expense tracker are low-competition entry points with clear affiliate programs.

### All-in-One / Solopreneur Ops (3 keywords, green)
Broad but low competition. Good pillar page candidates to anchor the freelancer operations section.
