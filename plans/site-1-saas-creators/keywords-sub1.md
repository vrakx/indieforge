## Priority Legend
- 🟢 **Green**: Low competition, good volume — quick wins. Prioritize these first.
- 🟡 **Yellow**: Moderate competition — worth targeting with strong content. Plan for longer tail.
- 🔴 **Red**: High competition — target only if you can differentiate significantly.

## Volume Source Notes
- Volumes estimated from SERP analysis (web_search) across 10+ queries covering: YouTube tools, streaming software, podcast editing/hosting, creator analytics, and monetization platforms.
- High-volume head terms (10K+) are competitive; mid-tail (3K–9K) with specific modifiers offer best ROI.
- Long-tail questions (e.g., "OBS vs Streamlabs", "Canva for thumbnails") are green-priority targets.

## Article Type Guide
- **Comparison**: Two or more tools side-by-side (best for: TubeBuddy vs VidIQ, Descript vs Audacity)
- **Review**: Single tool deep-dive with pros/cons, pricing, alternatives (best for: OpusClip, Pictory, Systeme.io)
- **Tutorial**: Step-by-step walkthrough with screenshots (best for: Canva thumbnails, Notion templates, funnel setup)
- **Roundup**: Multi-tool listicle organized by category/use-case (best for: "best tools for X", "free alternatives")
- **Guide**: Comprehensive evergreen content covering a topic end-to-end (best for: tech stack, analytics overview)
