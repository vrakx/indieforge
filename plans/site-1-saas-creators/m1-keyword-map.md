## Executive Summary

This document consolidates keyword research for Site #1 targeting SaaS tools for creators and solopreneurs. Five sub-niches were researched with a focus on **low-competition, high-intent keywords** suitable for quick ranking wins.

### Key Findings
- **72 of 104 keywords are green-priority** (difficulty ≤5) — these should be your Phase 1 content targets
- **32 keywords are yellow-priority** (difficulty 5-8) — good long-term targets with strong content differentiation
- **Top high-volume opportunity:** "best project management for freelancers" (14,800/mo) and "no code website builder" (18,000/mo)
- **Best quick wins:** Review keywords for tools like Rytr AI (diff:3), Reclaim.ai (diff:3), Kittl (diff:3)
- **Affiliate coverage:** 60+ SaaS tools with active affiliate programs identified across all sub-niches
