## Executive Summary

The SaaS tools market for creators, freelancers, and solopreneurs is the highest-ROI niche for our affiliate content strategy. With 20-30% recurring commissions from major platforms, AI-friendly content types, and medium competition that we can win through sub-niche specificity, this is the optimal first site.

- **Platform-specific stacks:** "Complete Tech Stack for Twitch Streamers in 2026"

## Content Production Cadence

**Phase 1 (May-June): Foundation** — 15 articles
- 5 comparison/VS articles (highest conversion)
- 3 tool reviews
- 3 tutorial/how-to guides
- 2 pillar pages
- 2 long-tail roundup posts

**Phase 2 (July-August): Authority Building** — 20-25 articles
- Continue comparison/VS at 2/month
- Add industry-specific stack guides
- Deep-dive tutorials for top-performing tools
- Seasonal content (back-to-school, holiday gifts)

**Phase 3 (September+): Scale & Diversify**
- Launch Site #2 in new niche
- Expand Site #1 to include video/Podcast content
- Build email list (lead magnet: "Creator Tech Stack Checklist")
